About us

We are a group of professional and experienced developers that have made it our goal to make coding easier to learn for kids.

Our passion has always been for coding, and now we want to create a love for programming amongst the new generation!

Reasons why to choose us:

  • We are teaching kids to think logically and develop an understanding of code!
  • Weekly meetings for different age groups
  • In depth tutorials on how to build your own games
